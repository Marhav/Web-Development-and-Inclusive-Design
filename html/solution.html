Our Solution

We recognize that it is not possible to solve the climate problem on our own. We hope that we can overcome this grand challenge if we cooperate. We must expand the awareness, such that everyone can contribute, instead of a few doing a lot. In our solution we want to bring awareness through challenges and competitions between friends through official events and individual performances. In addition, we seek to give people an incentive to be a part of the solution through internal challenges and meaningful awards that can be spent on benefits from our sponsors. All of this will be accomplished through our wonderful app, Climate+. Let us explain how we have realized this!

Earning Points

There are multiple ways to earn points. The method that accumulate the most points are the officially hosted events. These events can be anything imaginable as long as it contributes to a better environment e.g. planting trees or cleaning a riverside full of plastic to meeting up at protests. The amount of points each event rewards are decided by the time commitment and difficulty of the tasks set by the host. However, there are also other ways to obtain points individually. Will require you to connect Climate+ to various environmental friendly apps and reap additional points when using their services e.g. commuting by train instead of flight, in addition to scanning the receipt from recycling bottles.

Leaderboard

Now to the fun part; the competition between you and your friends. Once you receive points, they will count towards your ladder ranking. There will be official national and global ladders, but we highly recommend that you also create your own ladder with a group of friends. This way you can keep track of your friends' current rating and try to stay ahead. While there are no rewards for winning a private ladder, we imagine competetive people that wants some extra excitement could implement their own rewards privately.
Ladders are divided into seasons to provide people who falls behind a new chance to get to the top. After a new season has begun everybody start from scratch, with a rating of zero. However, your accumulated points so far will not be reset. You rating is purely based on how much points you have been able to acquire during the specific season.

Benefits

Finally, the points you have acquired will decide your rank in the ladder, points can also be spent on all kind of rewards that are provided by our sponsors in the app's benefit page. Moreover, you do not lose your rating when you spend points. As mentioned, you will keep your points after a season ends, however they will not count towards your rating in the upcoming seasons.
